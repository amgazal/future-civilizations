Future Civilizations Conference

April 18, 2026 • Cornell University

Conference Vision

The Future Civilizations Conference brings together faculty, students, and external speakers to explore how artificial intelligence, robotics, and autonomous systems are reshaping the foundations of human life. Using the idea of the “next human city” as a central lens, the conference examines how emerging technologies will transform social structures, economic systems, environmental sustainability, and even the possibility of off-world settlements.

Rather than treating the future as abstract speculation, this conference creates space for interdisciplinary dialogue that connects technical innovation with human values, ethical responsibility, and long-term societal planning.

What Will Happen at the Conference

Keynote & Speaker Addresses

Expert speakers from academia, research institutions, and industry will deliver talks addressing the technological, social, and ethical dimensions of AI-driven futures. These talks will provide both high-level vision and concrete examples of current research shaping tomorrow’s cities.

Panel Discussions

Moderated panel sessions will bring together speakers from different disciplines to debate real-world challenges, conflicting perspectives, and future policy questions. These discussions are designed to encourage critical thinking and cross-field collaboration.

Audience Q&A Sessions

Attendees will have opportunities to ask direct questions to speakers and panelists. These interactive sessions allow students and participants to engage deeply with ideas, challenge assumptions, and explore unanswered questions about AI, society, and civilization.

Live Demonstrations & Exhibitions

Research labs and creative teams will showcase hands-on demonstrations that highlight real applications of robotics, AI, and human–machine interaction. These exhibitions transform abstract concepts into tangible experiences.

Conference Panels

Designing the Autonomous City

This panel explores how AI and robotics will reshape architecture, transportation systems, urban infrastructure, and public spaces. Topics include smart mobility, automated logistics, and city-wide autonomous coordination.

Human Psychology, Purpose, and Social Life

This discussion examines how identity, creativity, emotional health, and social relationships evolve in environments deeply integrated with autonomous systems, from AI companions to algorithmic social platforms.

Sustainability & Agriculture

Panelists will explore AI-driven agriculture, resource management, food production systems, and environmental technologies that will be essential to sustaining growing urban populations.

Frontier Cities: Beyond Earth

This session investigates off-world settlements such as Mars, focusing on the technological, social, and governance challenges of building entirely new civilizations beyond Earth.

Speakers & Special Guests

The speaker lineup will feature a diverse group of professors, researchers, technologists, and industry leaders working at the intersection of artificial intelligence, robotics, urban systems, and sustainability.

Full speaker profiles and presentation topics will be announced closer to the conference date.
